Blueprint Ekosistem FM & Catering

Mind map level tinggi: 8 pilar dari data, proses, sampai value ke BOD.
EKOSISTEM FACILITY MANAGEMENT & CATERING
Perencanaan → Operasional → Kontrol Keuangan → Decision Support → Continuous Improvement
Dibangun sebagai mini-ERP khusus FM & catering, scalable untuk multi-site dan standar korporasi.
1
Data Backbone
master data & SSOT
Master Data Governance
  • Master item & kategori, satuan, supplier utama & alternatif.
  • Master site: kode site, alamat, pax, SLA.
  • Master recipe/BOM: bahan per porsi, yield, cost per portion.
  • Master manpower & asset register (alat & fasilitas).
Single Source of Truth (SSOT)
  • Satu sumber data untuk semua modul.
  • Awal: Google Sheets terstruktur & terkunci hak akses.
  • Tahap lanjut: database SQL (MySQL/PostgreSQL).
Standardisasi Kode
  • Format seragam: PR, PO, item, site, recipe code.
  • Mudah di-trace untuk kebutuhan audit & investigasi.
2
Process Layer
hulu → hilir
WMS (Warehouse)
  • Stock level, stock movement, stock opname.
  • FIFO/FEFO, batch & expiry control.
  • Repeat order otomatis berdasarkan kebutuhan & safety stock.
  • Multi-site stock visibility & transfer antar site.
Procurement
  • Flow PR → Review → Approval → PO → GRN → Invoice.
  • Approval matrix (limit per jabatan).
  • Vendor performance (OTIF, kualitas, harga).
  • Link ke WMS (stok) & ke finance (komitmen & pembayaran).
Finance & Budget
  • Budget per site & kategori cost.
  • Commitment vs actual vs variance.
  • Food cost & cost per pax per site/kontrak.
  • Early warning bila mendekati atau melewati budget.
Service Delivery
  • Catering: menu planning, meal order, portioning, waste.
  • Facility: cleaning checklist, quality inspection, work order.
  • Manpower scheduling sesuai SLA & pax.
3
Intelligence Layer
dashboard
Cost & Procurement Dashboard
  • Food cost & cost per pax per site.
  • Trend harga bahan & konsumsi.
  • Lead time PR → PO → GRN.
  • Vendor OTIF & ranking vendor.
Operational & Facility Dashboard
  • Jumlah meal served & waste/return meal.
  • Issue & complaint tracking per site.
  • Status preventive maintenance & downtime.
  • SLA kebersihan & service level facility.
Executive & Budget View
  • Budget vs actual vs commitment.
  • Deviation & root cause per kategori cost.
  • Benchmark antar site (cost, SLA, waste).
4
Output Layer
laporan
Daily Reports
  • Daily production & meal/pax report.
  • Ringkasan stock movement harian.
  • Status checklist cleaning & maintenance.
Weekly Reports
  • Rekap isu & komplain.
  • Vendor performance mingguan.
  • Trend sederhana cost & waste.
Monthly Management Reports
  • Executive summary per site & konsolidasi.
  • Cost summary & cost per pax.
  • SLA & KPI achievement.
  • CAPA & rekomendasi perbaikan.
5
Ekosistem Tambahan
premium layer
Menu Engineering & Nutrition
  • Recipe & costing engine (per porsi).
  • Menu cycle 7/14/30 hari per site.
  • Informasi nutrisi per menu/porsi.
  • Analisis menu: rating & waste.
Manpower & Workforce Planning
  • Headcount planning per site & shift.
  • Skill matrix & sertifikasi (expiry date).
  • Penjadwalan & rotasi shift.
  • Produktivitas & utilization per posisi.
Asset & Preventive Maintenance
  • Asset register lengkap (kitchen & facility).
  • Jadwal preventive maintenance.
  • Work order & downtime tracking.
  • Analisis penggantian asset (lifecycle).
Quality & Compliance
  • Audit digital (HACCP, HSE, 5R, dll).
  • Temuan & CAPA tracking.
  • Library SOP dengan versioning.
Customer / Client Portal
  • Form komplain & service request.
  • Rating menu & kebersihan.
  • Akses terbatas ke laporan & dashboard.
6
Integrasi & Arsitektur
nyawa sistem
Integrasi Data Internal
  • Semua modul baca/tulis ke SSOT.
  • Relasi antar tabel jelas (item–recipe–PO–stock).
  • Minim duplikasi & versi data ganda.
Stack Teknologi
  • Tahap awal: Google Workspace + Apps Script.
  • Tahap lanjut: Backend (Laravel/Node/Django) + DB SQL.
  • Frontend: web/PWA untuk site & HO.
Otomasi
  • Reminder sertifikasi & jadwal PM.
  • Summary harian via email/dashboard.
  • Snapshot data periodik untuk reporting.
Mini Data Warehouse
  • Database khusus analitik jangka panjang.
  • Analisis tren harga, cost, kinerja vendor & site.
7
Roadmap Teknologi
tahap 1–3
Tahap 1 (0–6 Bulan)
  • Standardisasi master data.
  • WMS dasar + PR/PO di Google Sheets.
  • Dashboard basic di Looker Studio.
Tahap 2 (6–18 Bulan)
  • Pembangunan web app internal / PWA.
  • Migrasi transaksi ke DB SQL.
  • Role-based access & UX lebih bagus.
Tahap 3 (18+ Bulan)
  • Arsitektur modular/microservice per domain.
  • Integrasi dengan HRIS, accounting, sistem klien.
  • Data warehouse & BI lebih advanced.
*Roadmap bisa dipakai sebagai bahan presentasi ke BOD & guideline tim IT.
8
Value untuk BOD
bahasa korporasi
Transparansi & Auditability
  • Trace: cost → menu → bahan → vendor → PO/PR.
  • Log aktivitas: siapa input/approve apa, kapan.
Control & Risk Management
  • Procurement & WMS saling mengunci.
  • Fraud & kebocoran cost lebih terjaga.
Scalability & Replicability
  • Desain sekali, bisa di-rollout ke banyak site.
  • Site baru = setup master data + template.
Decision Support & Branding
  • Keputusan berbasis data & simulasi, bukan feeling.
  • Menaikkan nilai perusahaan di mata klien/investor.
